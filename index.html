What is Vanshavli?

Vanshavli is a free Open-source genetic ancestry modeling tool that analyzes your DNA data to reveal deep ancestral components. Using advanced computational methods, we break down your genetic makeup into ancient population components that tell the story of human migration.

Our platform provides detailed insights into prehistoric populations that contribute to your genetic heritage, including hunter-gatherers, early farmers, and steppe pastoralists.
