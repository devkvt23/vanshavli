WHAT IS VANSHAVLI?

Vanshavli is a cutting-edge genetic ancestry modeling project that analyzes your DNA data to reveal deep ancestral components that shaped human populations across millennia. Using advanced computational methods, we break down your genetic makeup into ancient population components that tell the story of human migration and mixing.

Our platform goes beyond simple ethnicity estimates to provide detailed insights into the prehistoric populations that contribute to your genetic heritage, including hunter-gatherers, early farmers, and steppe pastoralists.
